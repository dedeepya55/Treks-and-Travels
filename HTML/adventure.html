ZIP LINING
A zip-line, zip line, zip-wire, flying fox, or death slide is a pulley suspended on a cable, usually made of stainless steel, mounted on a slope. It is designed to enable cargo or a person propelled by gravity to travel from the top to the bottom of the inclined cable by holding on to, or being attached to, the freely moving pulley.

ADVENTURES IN INDIA
Adventures in India offer a diverse array of thrilling experiences, thanks to the country's varied landscapes and rich natural resources. Whether you are an adrenaline junkie or someone seeking to explore the outdoors, India has something for everyone.

Trekking is one of the most popular adventure activities, with the Himalayan region offering some of the best treks in the world. From the challenging Roopkund Trek to the beautiful Valley of Flowers Trek, there are numerous routes that cater to all levels of trekkers. The Western Ghats also provide excellent trekking opportunities, particularly in the Sahyadri and Nilgiri ranges.

River rafting is another exciting adventure that draws many to India. The Ganges River in Rishikesh is renowned for its exhilarating rapids, making it a top destination for rafting enthusiasts. Additionally, the Zanskar River in Ladakh offers a thrilling rafting experience through deep gorges and stunning landscapes.

For those who prefer airborne adventures, paragliding in Bir Billing, Himachal Pradesh, is an unforgettable experience. Known as one of the best paragliding spots in the world, Bir Billing offers breathtaking views and ideal flying conditions. Similarly, Kamshet near Pune is a favored destination for paragliding, with its picturesque landscapes and favorable wind conditions.

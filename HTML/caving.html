OVERVIEW
Experience the thrill of exploration with the Meghalaya Caving Adventure Tour Package. This 7-night, 8-day journey through Meghalaya is priced at ₹25,000 per person (minimum of 4 persons). Your adventure begins in Shillong, where you'll arrive, transfer to your hotel, and enjoy a welcome dinner with a briefing about the upcoming activities. On Day 2, explore Shillong's attractions, including Elephant Falls and Shillong Peak, and prepare for the caving adventures ahead. Day 3 takes you to Siju, known for its impressive caves, where you'll spend the day exploring one of India’s longest and deepest caves. Return to Shillong for the night. On Day 4, travel to Cherrapunji to visit the Mawsmai Caves, renowned for their stunning formations, and return to Shillong in the evening. Day 5 is dedicated to exploring the Laitlum Canyons, offering panoramic views and unique rock formations. Spend the day hiking and enjoying the landscape before returning to Shillong. On Day 6, head to Jowai to explore the Nartiang Caves, known for their distinctive formations, and return to Shillong by evening. Day 7 provides a leisure day in Shillong to explore any remaining local sights or engage in optional activities. On the final day, check out from your hotel and transfer to the airport for your departure. The package includes accommodation, caving activities with professional guidance, local transportation, and specified meals. Exclusions cover airfare, personal expenses, travel insurance, and additional activities not listed.

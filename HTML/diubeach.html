OVERVIEW
Day-1 : Visit Jampore Beach and Devka Beach, two of the most popular beaches in Daman.Explore the Moti Daman Fort, a 16th-century Portuguese fort.Visit the Church of Our Lady of the Sea, a beautiful church with stunning architecture.Enjoy the evening at the local markets, shopping for souvenirs and trying local street food.
Day-2 : Try parasailing, car racing, or boating at the beach.Take a camel or horse ride along the beach.Visit the Lighthouse, which offers stunning views of the surrounding area.Enjoy the evening with a relaxing walk along the beach.
Day-3 : Take a day trip to Diu (around 659 km from Daman).Visit the Diu Fort, a historic fort with stunning architecture.Explore the beautiful Nagoa Beach and enjoy water sports.Visit the St. Paul's Church, a beautiful church with a rich history.
Day-4 : Spend the day relaxing at the beach or by the pool.Visit the local markets for some last-minute shopping.Try some of the local cuisine, such as chicken xacuti, chicken bullet, or butter-garlic tiger prawns.

OVERVIEW
     On the first day ,First pick up point is from Indian Museum Main Gate Near Park Street Metro, reporting time is 8:00 AM. and departure time is 8:30 AM ,Breakfast Tea      Break on the way. Reaching Hotel, which is located just opposite to the Sundarban Tiger Reserve surrounded by the village, forests, breeze of the river, the place where      you can enjoy a peaceful and pleasure weekend. A short introduction via boat to the Sundarban Bird Jungle with the lovely sunset view which is more mesmerizing after      the ‘sunsets’ we will back to the Hotel at 6 pm. Local folk dance followed by complimentary snacks Tea / Coffee.Bornfire & music winter season only. Dinner and Night      stay at Hotel.

     On the second day,The morning we will start Sundarban delta Safari through a boat.Start Jungle Safari guided by our Govt tourist Guide at Sajnekhali Tiger Reserve.We      will start our thrilling boat Safari through various rivers, Islands & small Creeks covering Pirkhali, Banbibi Varani, Sundarkhali, Khonakhali, Choragaji, Deualvarani,      SarakKhali 1 or 2, Gazikhali, Nobanki, Sudhanyakhali Watch Tower In this Tiger Reserve area for our share of viewing animals like spotted deer, crocodile, wild Boar,      snakes, water monitor lizard, different types of birds and also Royal Bengal Tiger ( if you are lucky).Visit Do-Banki Tiger Reserve Area & Watch Tower. The 896-meter      canopy walk at Do Banki gives you an opportunity to view wildlife from a netted enclosure.A Visit to Panchamukhni 5 River junction. After this thirilling sundarban      national park safari we will reach at Pakhiralay.Dinner will be served at Hotel

     On the third day,In the morning having breakfast we go to the local village walk for a couple of hours really fantastic, back to the Hotel than to go to visit Hamilton      Bungalow & Rabindranath Tagore Bungalow and next transfer by boat reach the car and head back to Kolkata, Lunch will be provided on the way.Reach Kolkata.We are      committed to give you better and best service, therefore, we request to you please co-operate with us, during the tour and make it unforgettable moments.

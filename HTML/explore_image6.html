OVERVIEW
     On the first day ,Make an early start for the day with a cup of tea or coffee before heading toward the Thiksey Monastery built in 1430 AD. At Thiksey Monastery,join the      mystical Morning Prayers session,be mesmerised by the sound of hundreds of young and old lamas chanting together to the amazing sound of their singing bowls.Later,      visit a local restaurant for a delicious Ladakhi breakfast and on the way back to leh visit Shey Gompa. Overnight stay at the hotel in Leh.

     On the second day, This day marks the commencement of the Snow Leopard Trek. Today, we have to make our way to Zingchen after having an early breakfast.The trek      on foot will begin from Zingchen, and we will head towards Rumbak valley, over a mild, gradual trail that climbs uphill. We will maintain a steady pace today, as the trail      is mostly ascending. It's important to observe meticulously, for your first sighting of the Snow Leopard will most likely be here in Rumbak valley.After lunch, you will      hike to a nearby viewpoint and scan for snow leopards and other wildlife. Dinner and overnight at the campsite.

     On the third and fourth day,Next two days will be spent in Hemis National Park, using your Rumbak camp as a base. Our guide will search the trails looking for signs of      Snow Leopards throughout the Rumbhak, Husing, & Tarbung valleys, in these area we can find around 9 to 10 different cats. After breakfast, our guide will make a      decision on where to take you for the day to trace Snow Leopards. Dinner & overnight stay at the camp in Rumbak Valley.

     On the fifth day,It's time to say farewell to the Ladakh Snow Leopard Summer Trek after having a wonderfull breakfast .Leave on time for Leh airport for onward journey      to Delhi.

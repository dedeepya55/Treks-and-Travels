The Great Himalayan National Park

Destination:Himachal Pradesh

Tour Name:The Great Himalayan National Park Tour Package

Tour Duration:04 Nights / 05 Days

Tour Price:11,500/- Per Person(Min-2 Person)

OVERVIEW
     On the first day ,Kharongcha gate trekking.Have breakfast at Tirthan Valley and then start the trek from Goshaini to the Gate at Kharongcha.Enjoy trekking to the GH      National Park, have lunch en route and you will visit Tirthan river for relaxing.The luxurious night camp is at Kharongcha gate with a lusious dinner and bonfire.

     On the second day ,Kharongcha to Rolla and then to Shilt.Strenuous trek to Shilt Thatch through the heavenly jungles, have a glance of the wild animals freely walking      near you.You will cross the thick jungle that is acting as a haven for Monal Pheasant.Have your lunch at Shilt before you start exploring the wildlife and other natural      wonders at thatch.

     On the third day, Shilt to Rolla.Cover 6 kilometers of jungle road, be lucky to watch many endangered birds like the Western Tragopan.The forests seen here are homes to      many rare speices of flora and fauna and you will be excited to share your time and space with the wonders of nature.Rolls is your destination and camping will be there at      night.

     Rolla to Tinder.The day will be packed with trekking to the Eco zone villages.You will be offered numerous photographic sights as well as glimpses of rural life in the hills      and also find time to marvel at the traditional architecture.Reach Tinder and relax afterward.

     On the fifth day,Return journey to Goshani.After a luscious breakfast, get ready for strolling down the beautiful village paths of the Tirthan Wildlife Sanctuary.Before      reaching Goshani, taste a sumptuous packed lunch while relaxing on the banks of Tirthan river.Get back to Aut where your tour gets wrapped up.

OVERVIEW
Day-1 : Check into your accommodation and get settled.Breakfast at Namaste Cafe - A popular spot right on Om Beach, offering a variety of breakfast options.Relax and explore the main Gokarna Beach. Enjoy the sun, sand, and sea.Lunch at Mantra Cafe - Located on Gokarna Beach, offering delicious food with a great view.Take a short walk or auto ride to Kudle Beach to watch the beautiful sunset.Dinner at Cafe 1987 - Known for its relaxed ambiance and a variety of dishes.
Day-2 : Start your day with a yoga session on the beach.Breakfast at Ganga Cafe - Enjoy a healthy breakfast with a view.Visit Om Beach - Named for its shape, Om Beach is perfect for swimming and sunbathing.Lunch at Om Beach Cafe - Located right on the beach, offering a range of seafood and local dishes.Water Activities at Om Beach - Engage in activities like kayaking, paddleboarding, or banana boat rides.
Day-3 : Visit the nearby Beach Houses - They offer a relaxed beachside dining experience. Breakfast at Beach House 1 - A popular spot on the beach, offering a variety of breakfast options.Lunch at Beach House 2 - Located on the beach, offering a range of seafood and local dishes.Dinner at Beach House 3 - Known for its relaxed ambiance and a variety of dishes.

KOVALAM BEACH

Destination : Kerala

Tour Name : Kerala Tour Package

Tour Duration : 04 Nights / 05 Days

OVERVIEW
Day-1 : Check into your hotel and get settled.Enjoy a relaxed breakfast with a view of the sea.Visit the iconic lighthouse, climb to the top for panoramic views, and enjoy the beach.A popular spot for seafood and traditional Kerala cuisine.Spend the evening at Hawa Beach, known for its beautiful sunset views. Enjoy a variety of local and international dishes.
Day-2 : Start your day with some water activities. Surfing lessons are available for beginners.Visit Samudra Beach a quieter part of Kovalam, ideal for a peaceful afternoon.Offering a mix of Indian and international cuisine.Kovalam is famous for its Ayurvedic treatments. Book a session at a reputable spa.Dinner at Bait Restaurant located at the Taj Green Cove Resort, offering a luxurious dining experience.
Day-3 : Visit Padmanabhapuram Palace - A day trip to this historical palace, known for its traditional Kerala architecture.Breakfast en route - Try a local café for a traditional Kerala breakfast.Return to Kovalam and relax.Lunch at Spice Village Restaurant - Enjoy authentic Kerala flavors.Attend a Kathakali dance performance to experience Kerala's traditional dance form.A fine dining experience with a view of the Arabian Sea.
Day-4 : Visit Kovalam Beach. Enjoy a sunset view of the sea and enjoy the beach.Breakfast at a local café.Lunch at the local restaurant.Dinner at a traditional Kerala restaurant.A beautiful sunset view of the sea.

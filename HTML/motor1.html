OVERVIEW
A Leh-Ladakh trip is a captivating journey that spans 07 nights and 08 days, covering destinations such as Leh, Nubra Valley, Pangong Lake, Khardung La, and Tso Moriri. The trip starts with your arrival in Leh, where you'll spend the first day acclimatizing to the high altitude and exploring local sights like the Shanti Stupa, Leh Palace, and bustling local markets.

On the second day, you’ll dive into Leh’s rich cultural tapestry with a full day of sightseeing, visiting iconic sites such as Hemis Monastery, Thiksey Monastery, Shey Palace, and Stok Palace. The third day takes you on a scenic drive to Nubra Valley via Khardung La Pass, one of the highest motorable roads in the world. In Nubra Valley, you’ll explore Diskit Monastery and enjoy a unique Bactrian camel ride at the Hunder Sand Dunes.

Returning to Leh on the fourth day, you can visit Samstanling Monastery, taking in more of the region's stunning landscapes and cultural heritage. Day five’s highlight is the breathtaking drive to Pangong Lake via Chang La Pass, where you’ll spend the night in lakeside camps, experiencing the lake’s changing colors and tranquil beauty.

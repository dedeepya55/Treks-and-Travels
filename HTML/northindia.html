NORTH INDIA

North India! A destination pulsating with extraordinary attractions. Endowed with sheer natural beauty and a rich cultural heritage, North India beckons you for a travel experience of a lifetime.From the historic, majestic, and celestial destinations such as Delhi, Himachal Pradesh, Jammu & Kashmir, Rajasthan, Punjab, Haryana, Uttar Pradesh, and Uttaranchal, to the fascinating attractions like world-famous Heritage sites, hill stations, adventure sports, scenic beauty, cruises, wildlife, and pilgrimage sites, North India has something for everyone.

TEMPLES

North India is home to numerous ancient and revered temples, showcasing the region's rich cultural heritage.

WILD LIFE

North India is home to a diverse range of wildlife, including the majestic Bengal tiger.

ADVENTURES

North India offers a plethora of adventure activities, including paragliding in Bir, Himachal Pradesh.

Stunning Hill Stations

Abundant hill stations in North India spread all across the region that makes for a tranquil and picturesque destination for tourists. Kashmir has some of the most beautiful hill stations in India which include Pahalgam, Gulmarg, Srinagar, and Sonmarg. In Himachal Pradesh, tourists can be spoilt for choice with these fantastic hill stations in North India which include Shimla, Manali, and Dalhousie. Mussoorie and Nainital are among the most popular hill stations in North India. Almora and McLeodganj are among the lesser crowded hill stations in North India.

Powerfull Temples

Temples in North India! The region is home to some of the most revered and awe-inspiring temples in the country. Here are some of the most famous and sacred temples in North India: Kashi Vishwanath Temple, Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh. Amarnath Cave Temple, Jammu and Kashmir. Vaishno Devi Temple, Jammu and Kashmir. Golden Temple, Amritsar, Punjab. Kedarnath Temple, Uttarakhand. Badrinath Temple, Uttarakhand.

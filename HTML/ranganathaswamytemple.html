OVERVIEW
Sri Ranganathaswamy Temple, located in Srirangam near Tiruchirappalli in Tamil Nadu, is a prominent and ancient Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu. Renowned for its vast and intricate architecture, the temple is a major pilgrimage site for followers of the Vaishnavism tradition. It is one of the largest functioning Hindu temples in the world, covering an extensive area with numerous halls, towers, and enclosures. The temple is particularly famous for its stunning gopurams (gateway towers) and its rich array of sculptures and frescoes. It is also a central site for the annual Ranganathaswamy festival, drawing thousands of devotees and tourists who come to marvel at its spiritual ambiance and architectural splendor.

OVERVIEW
Embark on an adrenaline-pumping journey with the Mysore Skydiving Adventure Tour Package. This 4-night, 5-day adventure, priced at ₹25,000 per person (minimum 4 persons), offers a thrilling skydiving experience coupled with the exploration of Mysore’s rich heritage. Upon arrival in Mysore, you will transfer to your hotel and enjoy a welcome dinner with a briefing about the skydiving activities ahead. On Day 2, head to the skydiving center for an exhilarating tandem jump, where you will be guided by professional instructors to experience the thrill of freefalling and parachuting over the scenic landscapes of Mysore. After the jump, spend the rest of the day exploring Mysore's attractions, including the Mysore Palace and Brindavan Gardens, before returning to your hotel. Day 3 is dedicated to another skydiving session, allowing you to further refine your skills and enjoy the breathtaking aerial views. In the afternoon, visit the Chamundi Hills and St. Philomena's Church. On Day 4, enjoy a leisure day where you can choose optional activities such as visiting the Mysore Zoo or taking a heritage walk through the city. In the evening, participate in a farewell dinner to celebrate your adventurous experience. On the final day, check out from your hotel and transfer to the airport or railway station for your onward journey. The package includes comfortable accommodation, all skydiving activities with professional guidance, local transportation, and specified meals. Exclusions cover airfare or transportation to/from Mysore, personal expenses, travel insurance, and any additional activities not listed.

OVERVIEW
Somnath Temple, located in Prabhas Patan near Veraval in Saurashtra, Gujarat, is one of the most revered and ancient temples in India. Dedicated to Lord Shiva, it is renowned for its architectural grandeur and historical significance. The temple's rich history dates back to ancient times, with several reconstructions over centuries due to various invasions. It is believed to be the first of the twelve Jyotirlinga shrines dedicated to Shiva, making it a pivotal site for pilgrims and tourists alike. The temple stands as a symbol of resilience and devotion, attracting countless visitors who come to experience its spiritual and historical legacy.

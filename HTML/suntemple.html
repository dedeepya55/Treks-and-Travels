OVERVIEW
The Sun Temple, also known as the Surya Mandir, is located in Konark, Odisha. This 13th-century temple is an architectural marvel and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Built in the shape of a colossal chariot with intricately carved stone wheels, pillars, and walls, the temple is dedicated to the Sun God, Surya. It is designed to resemble Surya's chariot, drawn by seven horses. The Sun Temple is not only a symbol of India's rich cultural heritage but also a testament to the advanced architectural and artistic skills of the time. Visitors are captivated by its stunning sculptures and the awe-inspiring grandeur that has withstood the test of time.

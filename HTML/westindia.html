West India! West India comprises the states of Goa, Gujarat, and Maharashtra, and is a treasure trove of rich cultural heritage, stunning natural beauty, and vibrant cities.Western India houses a variety of flora and fauna as it is home to the largest desert in India, the Thar Desert and the coastal regions. This part is home to the most happening place in India, Goa.

TEMPLES

West India is home to a rich cultural heritage, and its temples are a testament to its spiritual significance.

WILD LIFE

West India is home to a diverse range of wildlife, with over 40 mammal species and nearly 450 bird species.

ADVENTURES

West India is home to some of the most stunning and diverse beaches in the country. From the scenic shores of Maharashtra to the tropical paradises of Goa and Daman and Diu, there's a beach for every kind of traveler.

Powerfull Temples

West India, comprising the states of Maharashtra, Gujarat, and Goa, is home to a rich cultural heritage and a plethora of ancient temples that showcase the region's architectural, artistic, and spiritual grandeur. Wild Wonders Await: West India's Untamed Beauty

West India, comprising the states of Maharashtra, Gujarat, and Goa, is home to a diverse range of wildlife, thanks to its varied landscape and climate. Gir National Park

Gir National Park, also known as Sasan Gir, is a forest national park, and wildlife sanctuary near Talala Gir in Gujarat, India.

Marine National Park

The Marine National Park is located in the Gulf of Kutch, in the Devbhumi Dwarka district of Gujarat, India.

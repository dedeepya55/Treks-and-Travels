OVERVIEW
Embark on the Kerala Adventure Extravaganza Tour Package with a 7-night, 8-day journey through the vibrant landscapes of Kerala, priced at ₹25,000 per person (minimum 4 persons). Upon arrival, you’ll transfer to your hotel and enjoy a relaxing evening with a welcome dinner and briefing about the upcoming adventures. On Day 2, travel to Kovalam Beach for an exhilarating parasailing experience and enjoy breathtaking views of the Arabian Sea before heading back to your hotel. The next day, depart for Munnar, where you'll explore lush greenery and tea plantations on a guided trekking adventure. Return to Munnar in the evening for a restful night.Day 4 is dedicated to zip-lining at adventure parks in Munnar, followed by exploration of the area. On Day 5, head to Thekkady for a thrilling white water rafting experience on the Pamba River amidst beautiful surroundings. You’ll stay overnight in Thekkady. Day 6 takes you to Wayanad for an exciting jungle safari in the Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary, where you’ll have the chance to spot diverse wildlife. Return to your accommodation in Wayanad for a relaxing evening. Day 7 offers a leisurely day to unwind or engage in optional activities such as nature walks or local cultural tours, followed by a farewell dinner. On your final day, check out and transfer to the airport for your departure. This package includes accommodation, all specified adventure activities with professional guidance, local transportation, and meals.

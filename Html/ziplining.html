OVERVIEW
Embark on an exhilarating journey with the Kerala Zip Lining Adventure Tour Package. This 4-night, 5-day adventure, priced at ₹25,000 per person (minimum 4 persons), offers thrilling zip lining experiences amidst Kerala’s lush landscapes and serene backwaters. Upon arrival in Kerala, you will transfer to your hotel and enjoy a welcome dinner with a briefing about the activities ahead. On Day 2, head to a famous zip lining spot where you will glide over picturesque valleys and dense forests, guided by professional instructors. After the adrenaline-pumping activity, spend the rest of the day exploring local attractions and enjoying Kerala’s natural beauty before returning to your hotel. Day 3 is dedicated to another zip lining session, allowing you to further enjoy the breathtaking aerial views and the thrill of high-speed gliding. In the afternoon, visit nearby attractions such as tea plantations or spice gardens. On Day 4, enjoy a leisure day where you can choose optional activities such as a houseboat cruise on the backwaters, visiting wildlife sanctuaries, or indulging in Ayurvedic spa treatments. In the evening, participate in a farewell dinner to celebrate your adventurous experience. On the final day, check out from your hotel and transfer to the airport or railway station for your onward journey. The package includes comfortable accommodation, all zip lining activities with professional guidance, local transportation, and specified meals. Exclusions cover airfare or transportation to/from Kerala, personal expenses, travel insurance, and any additional activities not listed.
